About

The Green Project is a community effort to archive and document Xbox One's amazing hardware and software. We hope to pass this information, assets and knowledge on to the next generation of hackers so that they can go forth into their forebears' footsteps and break the ridiculous bonds Xbox put on their 8th generation consoles.
